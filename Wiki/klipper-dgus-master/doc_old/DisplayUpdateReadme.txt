Display Software Update
-----------------------

1)  Prepare MicroSD Card 

    Warning all content of the MicroSD card is deleted!
    
    Windows:
    format <MicroSD Disk Letter> /q /fs:fat32 /a:4096

    Linux:
    <TODO>


2)  Copy DWIN_SET Folder to MicroSD

    Copy the DWIN_SET folder to the MicroSD card


3)  Update Display

    Put MicroSD to Display.
    (Contacts in direction of display, little notch right)


4)  Power On Display 
    (Connect USB-TTL, to some Host Device)

4.1)    Display Shows a blue Mask and some text,
        Second line shows "SD Card Process..."
        wait till second line switches to:

        "SD Card Process... END!"

5)  Power Display

6)  Remove MicroSD from Display
